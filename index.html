Who this is built for

Big Five (age 19): Extraversion — exceptionally high (enthusiasm + assertiveness both exceptional) · Openness — exceptionally high (intellect + openness both exceptional) · Neuroticism — very low (volatility exceptionally low) · Conscientiousness — average, with industriousness moderately low · Agreeableness — moderately low, politeness low.

Translation: you start fast, love people and novelty, don't feel guilt or deadline fear, and won't grind on willpower. So this system never asks for willpower, never uses guilt, and feeds you novelty and competition on purpose.

The seven rules

1 · The floor is sacred; the ceiling is optional.The Minimum Viable Day (~25 min) is the only real commitment. Big days are bonus points, not obligations. A system you can satisfy on your worst day is a system you can't fail.
2 · Never miss twice.Missing one day is noise — with your volatility, it won't even sting, and it shouldn't. Missing two in a row is a trend. Day-after-a-miss is an automatic MVD-only day with a +15 comeback bonus. Coming back IS the win.
3 · People are the engine.Book live Spanish speaking in advance (paid + scheduled = happens). Teach what you learn. Report your week to someone. Solo silent grinding is your failure mode — social structure is your fuel.
4 · Compete with last week, not with an ideal.You're assertive and competitive — aim it at the scoreboard. One number per week; beat your PR. Ranks: Bronze 400 · Silver 550 · Gold 700.
5 · Novelty gets a slot, not a veto.Your openness will keep generating new obsessions. Good — they go in the Wildcard slot (30 min weekdays, long block Saturday). New idea mid-block? Write it on the capture list; it gets its audition Saturday. The plan bends so it doesn't break.
6 · Decide once, on Sunday.Low industriousness dies by a thousand daily decisions. The Sunday review (30 min) sets the week's 3 targets, books the speaking sessions, and picks the wildcard — weekdays just execute the default week.
7 · Environment beats intention.Phone out of the room for the morning sprint. Spanish app on the home screen; feeds off it. Foundations materials open on the desk before bed. Make the right thing the lazy thing.
8 · The Witness Rule — the audit's verdict.Six years of your own records say one thing: capture survives, closure dies — unless a human is attached. Every system you kept had people in it; every solo system died in about three weeks. So nothing here counts as a commitment until it has a witness and a date. The nightly check-in is the witness of last resort: answering it IS the closure step.
