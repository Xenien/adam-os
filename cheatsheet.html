The 8 formatting rules ATS parsers punish

  1. Use standard section headings — Summary, Experience, Education, Skills. Creative headings ("My Journey") don't get parsed.
  2. Email and phone near the top, as plain text — never inside an image or header/footer block.
  3. Date ranges on every role (e.g. 2021 – 2024). Missing dates can drop the whole entry.
  4. Match the posting's exact wording. If they say "project management," don't only write "managed projects."
  5. Quantify. Numbers, percentages, dollar amounts — at least 3–5 measurable results.
  6. Start bullets with action verbs — Led, Built, Increased — never "Responsible for."
  7. Keep it 400–800 words. Thin resumes don't rank; bloated ones bury the signal.
  8. No tables, columns, text boxes, or graphics — many parsers read them out of order or not at all.
