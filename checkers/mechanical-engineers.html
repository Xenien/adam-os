3 resume tips for mechanical engineers

  1. Name your CAD and analysis tools on the bullets, not just the skills list. "Designed injection-molded housings in SolidWorks" ties the tool to real work; a bare tools list without context ranks lower with humans even when it passes the software.
  2. Spell out your quality and process methods. If you ran root cause analysis, wrote it up as CAPA, or worked in a Six Sigma or lean manufacturing environment, say so in those exact words — process vocabulary is screened as heavily as software names.
  3. Quantify in engineering units and money. Tolerance achieved, scrap rate reduced, cycle time cut, cost per unit saved, parts released to production. "Cut machining cycle time 18%, saving $240K/yr" survives screening and tells the reviewing engineer you measure what you build.
