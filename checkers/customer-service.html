3 resume tips for customer service

  1. Name your tools, even the "obvious" ones. Zendesk, Salesforce, a phone system, a POS — whatever you actually used. Screening software searches for product names, and "handled customer inquiries" matches none of them.
  2. Lead with your numbers — support is full of them. Tickets or calls per day, CSAT score, first-response time, resolution rate, retention saves. "Resolved 60+ tickets daily at 94% CSAT" survives screening and instantly separates you from the pile.
  3. Match the posting's label for the role. Companies variously say customer service, customer support, customer success, and customer experience — and screen on their own term. Use the posting's wording for the same work you've always done.
