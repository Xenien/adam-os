3 resume tips for marketers

  1. Optimize your resume like a landing page. The posting is your keyword research — mirror its exact terms ("demand generation," not "getting leads") in your Skills section and bullets, wherever they're true of your work.
  2. Name the platforms. Google Analytics, Google Ads, HubSpot, Mailchimp, Salesforce — recruiters search by tool name. "Managed our martech stack" matches nothing; the product names match everything.
  3. Lead every bullet with the metric. Conversion lift, ROAS, open rates, pipeline sourced, audience growth. You'd never report a campaign without numbers — don't report your career without them either.
